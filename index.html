Blue Origin

suborbital flight

Blue Origin's New Shepard suborbital rocket on July 20, 2021, for the first time in the company's history, brought a capsule with people to space altitude, with the world's richest man Jeff Bezos on board.

New Shepard is 18 m high and 4 m wide - a reusable spacecraft. The booster system lifts the sealed passenger vessel to a height of about 76 km. The impulse brings the capsule up into space.

After that, she descends to the ground under three parachutes. The launch vehicle is also returning home.
